How the Model Works

End-to-end workflow of the machine learning based energy prediction system

Input data is validated, cleaned, and structured to match the trained model format.

Step 3: XGBoost Prediction

The trained XGBoost regression model learns complex feature interactions and predicts energy consumption.

Step 4: Energy Output

Final predicted energy consumption is displayed instantly in kilowatt-hours (kWh).
